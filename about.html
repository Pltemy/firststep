Our Story

A Trusted Name in Basic Education

First Step School has served families in Teshie Nungua Estates for more than two and a half decades. What began as a small, caring nursery has grown into a respected centre for nursery, kindergarten and primary education, proudly preparing generations of children for success in junior high school and in life.

We believe every child deserves a quality beginning. Our high standards and rich curriculum, delivered by experienced teachers in a safe and nurturing environment, give pupils the confidence and skills they need to thrive.
