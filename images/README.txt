FIRST STEP SCHOOL — IMAGE GUIDE
=================================

This site references images from the "images/" folder. Add your own
photos with the EXACT filenames below and they will appear automatically.

  logo.png        — School logo (square, used in header & footer)
  hero.jpg        — Large banner photo on the home page (wide)
  about.jpg       — About / learning photo (used on Home + About)
  campus.jpg      — School building / campus exterior
  activities.jpg  — Classroom or activities photo
  principal.jpg   — Head of School portrait (tall)
  gallery1.jpg … gallery6.jpg — Gallery photos (landscape recommended)

Tips
----
- Until a photo is added, an elegant sea-blue placeholder is shown.
- Recommended widths: hero ~1600px, gallery/campus ~800px, logo ~256px.
- Use .jpg (or .png for the logo). If you change the extension, also
  update the matching <img src="..."> in the HTML files.
