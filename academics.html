Stages of Learning

Our Academic Programs

Each stage is designed to meet children where they are and move them forward with confidence.

01

Creche

A safe, affectionate setting where toddlers explore through guided play, music and movement.

02

Nursery

Early language, counting, rhymes and social skills built through hands-on, joyful activities.

03

Kindergarten (KG1–KG2)

Reading readiness, phonics, writing and early numeracy that prepare children for primary school.

04

Primary (Basic 1–6)

English, Mathematics, Science, Our World, Religious & Moral Education, ICT, Creative Arts and more — aligned with the Ghanaian basic education curriculum.

Our Approach

Beyond the Textbook

We blend a strong academic core with activities that develop creativity, teamwork and curiosity. Small class sizes mean teachers truly know each child and tailor support to help every pupil succeed.

  • Phonics-based early reading
  • Hands-on maths and science
  • ICT and digital literacy
  • Music, art, sports & cultural activities
  • Regular assessment and parent updates
